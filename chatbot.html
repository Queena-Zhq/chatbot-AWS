Careers Service Chatbot

This is User Guidance. This careers chatbot is aiming at providing online services for students before they go to the Career Office. For start, you can type "Hello" to start a conversation, or type "Help" directly for service options. You can also ask various questions about careers yourselves. Sometimes the bot cannot understand your questions, you can try to change some of the keywords, and I will also analyse the reasons. The following lists are the main services the bot provides.
